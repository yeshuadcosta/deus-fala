THE MEMORARE

Intercession To Our Lady

Hail, holy Queen, Mother of mercy, our life, hail our sweetness
and our hope. To thee do we cry, poor banished children of Eve.
To thee do we send up our sighs, mourning and weeping in this
valley of tears. Turn, then, most gracious advocate, thine eyes
of mercy toward us, and after this, our exile, show unto
us the blessed fruit of thy womb, Jesus. O clement,
O loving, O sweet Virgin Mary.



V. Pray for us, O holy Mother of God.
R. That we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ.
AMEN
