REGINA COELI

Recited during the Easter Season

O Queen of Heaven, rejouce! Alleluia.
For He Whom you did merit to bear. Alleluia.
Has risen as he said. Alleluia.
Pray for us to God. Alleluia.

V. Rejoice and be glad, O Virgin Mary, Alleluia.
R. For the Lord has risen indeed, Alleluia.

Let us Pray

O God, Who gave joy to the world through the resurrection
of Your Son Our Lord Jesus Christ, grant that we may obtain,
through His Virgin Mary,ever Virgin, the joys of everlasting life.
Through the same Christ Our Lord. Amen
