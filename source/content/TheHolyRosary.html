The Holy Rosary

The Greatest Prayer of Intercession to The Blessed Virgin Mary

We begin the roary with The Sign of The Cross...
Followed by the Apostles' Creed. After which, we recite One Our Father and Three Hail Marys
The Sign Of The Cross
The Apostle's Creed
The Nicene Creed
The Our Father
Hail Mary
Glory Be
We choose the Mysteries of The Rosary based on the Day of the Week.
Each Mystery consists of Ten Decades. We contemplate on the event of the Mystery at Each Decade.
At each decade, we recite an Our Father follwed by Ten Hail Marys with The Sign of the Cross and The Glory before and after each decade of the rosary respectively.
