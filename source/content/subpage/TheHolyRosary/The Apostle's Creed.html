THE APOSTLE'S CREED

The Proffesion Of Our Faith

I believe in God, The Father almighty,
creator of Heaven and Earth
I believe in Jesus Christ,
His Only Son, Our Lord Who was
conceived by the Power of The
Holy Spirit, Born of The Virgin
Mary, Suffered on the Pontius
Pilate, Was Crucified, Died,
and was Buried. He descended
into Hell And on the third day,
he rose again from the Dead
He was seated at the right hand
of God: The Father Almighty
He will come again to judge
The Living and the Dead
I believe in the Holy Spirit
The Holy Catholic Church
The Communion Of Saints
The Forgiveness of Saints
The Resurrection of Body And
Life everlasting
Amen
