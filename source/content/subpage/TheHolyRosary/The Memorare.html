THE MEMORARE

Intercession To Our Lady

Remember the Most Gracious Virgin Mary
That never was it known that anyone who
fled to Her protection and Sought Thy
Intercession was left unaided
Inspired by this confidence, I fly unto Thee
O Virgin of Virgins, My Mother
To thee do I Come, sinful and sorrowful
O Mother of The Word Incarnate
Despise not my Petition
But in Your Mercy Hear and Answer Me.
Amen

V. O Mary conceived without sin.
R. Pray for Us who recourse to Thee
Amen
