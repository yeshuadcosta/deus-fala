ACT OF SPIRITUAL COMMUNION

My Jesus, I believe that You are truly present in
the most Holy Sacrament. I Love You above
all things and I desire to recive You
into my Soul. Since I cannot recieve you
Sacramentally, come at least spiritually
into my Heart. I embrace You as if You were
already there and Unite nyself wholly to You.
Never permit me to be separated from You
AMEN
