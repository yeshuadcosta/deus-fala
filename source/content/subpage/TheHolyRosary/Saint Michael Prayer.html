PRAYER TO SAINT MICHAEL

Prayer for Protection from the Saint Michael the Archangel



Saint Michael, The Archangel
defend us on the Day of Battle
Be our Safe Guard against the
Wickedness and Snares Of The Devil
May God Rebuke Him, we Humbly Pray
and do thou, O Prince of the
heavenly hosts, by the power
of God, Cast into hell Satan,
and all the evil spirits,
who prowl about the world
seeking the ruin of souls.
Amen

V. Mary Queen of the Most Holy Angels.
R. Pray for Us.
Amen
