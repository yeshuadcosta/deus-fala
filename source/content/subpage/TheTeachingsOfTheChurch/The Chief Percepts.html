THE CHIEF PERCEPTS

1. To keep the Sundays and Holy Days of Obligation Holy by hearing Mass and resting from seville work.
2. To keep the days of Fasting and Abstinence appointed by the Church
3. To go to Confession at least once a year
4. To receive the Blessed Sacrament
5. To contribute to the support of Our Priests
6. Not to Marry with certain degrees of kindred without Dispension
