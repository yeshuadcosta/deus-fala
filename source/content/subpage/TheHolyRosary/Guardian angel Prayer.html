GUARDIAN ANGEL'S PRAYER

Prayer for protection from one's Guardian Angel

Prayer during the day

Angel of God, my Guardian Angel Dear,
To whom God's Love commits me here;
Ever this Day be at my side;
To light and guard,
To rule and guide
Amen

Prayer during the night

Angel of God, my Guardian Angel Dear,
To whom God's Love commits me here;
Ever this night be at my side;
To light and guard,
To rule and guide
Amen
